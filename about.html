Our Story

Computer Solutions was founded in 2011 by Chaiyz Brown in Keystone Heights, Florida, with a simple mission: be your complete source for computer sales, service, and repair. What started as a passion for technology has grown into a trusted local business serving the Keystone Heights community and surrounding areas.

We believe in giving every customer an honest assessment, clear options, and quality work at fair prices. Whether it's a home PC, business laptop, or an LED/LCD/DLP television, every device that comes through our doors receives the same care and attention to detail.

Over the years, we've expanded from basic repairs to a full range of services including computer sales (new and refurbished), virus removal, data recovery, and business IT support. Our A+ BBB rating and loyal customer base speak to our commitment to doing things right.
